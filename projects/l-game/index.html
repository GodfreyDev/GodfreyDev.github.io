L Game

Discord Activity for fast, tactical matches

This Discord Activity adapts the classic L Game into a quick, turn-based challenge that runs right inside a voice channel.

Discord Activity Turn-Based Multiplayer Web

Overview

The goal is simple: place your L-shaped piece and a neutral block each turn to leave your opponent without any legal moves. The activity focuses on quick setup, clear turn states, and smooth rematches.

Key Features

  • Room-based flow with lobby, settings, and quick rematch support.
  • Turn indicators, history panel, and confirm/undo safeguards.
  • Clean visual hierarchy for at-a-glance board reads.
